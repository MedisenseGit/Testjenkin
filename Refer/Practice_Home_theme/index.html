WHAT IS PRACTICE

"Join the Network, Be A Brand & Leave A Legacy"

What is PRACTICE?

PRACTICE is the simplest, smart, secured & connected medical software application designed for doctors to

(a) Manage their medical practice

(b) Seek opinion & support from other experts in complex cases

(c) Teach &Learn while you practice – Post &Read Content. Viz. Surgical videos, challenging cases, Fellowship& job opportunities, CMEs, Conferences & more.


Who is eligible for membership ?

Doctors, Medical students.


What are the Membership Types ?

Standard membership :

You can manage your Practice, request for expertise from fellow doctors, view/comment/share medical content, surgical videos from experts, Apply for jobs/fellowships/CMEs/Conferences .

Premium Membership :

In addition to the above, you can post content that will reach all the members, Create your own followers group and communicate with the group securely. Enable your group to send you appointment/Expertise requests.


How to Access PRACTICE ?

  1. Choose – Standard or Premium
  2. Register – Provide your personal & medical education details and register.
  3. Login – Enter the newage healthcare management.


System Requirements : No Installation needed.

Web users : Open internet explorer, Google Chrome or Mozilla Firefox.



What is it that you can achieve with PRACTICE ?

All-in-one solution packed with modern features like:

Practice & Appointment Management

A user-friendly application which helps to digitalize all the patient records & schedule appointments, upload and manage patient records, prescriptions and medical reports. In addition to this the doctors can also request for expertise from the other specialists in state of confusion with the line of treatment to clear the confusion & help to take the right decisions.

Medical Professional Networking Platform

It allows you to create your own professional network and also allows you to connect with all the specialists across the country, share knowledge, request expertise, send/receive medical cases and more.

Medical Events

Stay up to date with all the latest and upcoming National & International
CMEs & conference invites, Fellowship & Training programs, Overseas Job opportunities and more.

Medical Gallery

Access to all the Blogs, Articles, Surgical Videos and Latest advancements in medical technology updated by the Top Specialists across the country. The posts reach out to 1,50,000+ doctors & 5,00,000+ patients worldwide.

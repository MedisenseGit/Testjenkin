WHAT IS PRACTICE

"Engaging physicians in healthcare revolution"

What is PRACTICE?

PRACTICE is a web and smart phone based application for doctors’ to manage their medical practice at their fingertips and is an all-in-one solution packed with modern features which enable them to

(a) Join the professional network of doctors’ to connect with all the specialists across the country

(b) Manage Appointments

(c) Create & manage patient records

(d) Register for CMEs & Conferences

(e) Apply for fellowship & jobs around the world

(f) Enhance medical education & contribute to medical community

It’s a digital era solution for doctors’ which allows them to completely digitize the patient records, encrypt the information & store it in cloud – where they can access the dashboard anywhere using PC or mobile phone


Who is eligible for membership ?

Medical students, GPs & super specialists


What are the Membership Types ?

Standard membership :

Eligibility: General practitioners / Junior doctors/ Medical post graduate students
Here, you can manage your appointments, patient records, send medical cases to peer doctors among your connections, request for expertise from experts on complex cases, view/comment/share medical contents like blogs, videos, latest advancements in medicine posted by experts, Apply for Jobs / Fellowships / CMEs / Conferences and more

Premium Membership :

Eligibility: Super specialists/ Hospitals
Here, In addition to the above standard features , you can receive medical cases from peer doctors & referring partners , post contents like blogs, videos and more that will reach all the members, Create your own followers group and communicate with the group securely. It also enables your group members to send you Appointment / Expertise requests.


How to Access PRACTICE ?

  1. Choose – Standard or Premiu
  2. Register – Provide your personal & medical education details and register.
  3. Login – Enter the new age healthcare management.


System Requirements : No Installation needed.

Web users : Open internet explorer, Google Chrome or Mozilla Firefox.

Mobile users: iOS – App store (PRACTICE standard/PRACTICE premium)
Android – Google play store (PRACTICE standard/PRACTICE premium)

A user-friendly application which helps to digitize all the patient records & schedule appointments, upload and manage patient records, prescriptions and medical reports.
